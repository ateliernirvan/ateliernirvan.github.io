O nás

Hire for attitude - Look for people who share your values and enthusiasm. Skills can be taught- mindset can't Trust your people from day one - If you wait for them to "earn" it, you're starting off on the wrong foot. Genuine trust fuels creativity and accountability. Set your expectations - Clarity in goals eliminates guesswork and ensures everyone knows what success looks like. Set them up for success - You can't expect great work if you don't give your team the support and resources they need to succeed. Let them get on with it - Micromanagement kills motivation. Give people the freedom to innovate, and they'll surprise you with what they can accomplish Measure outcomes, not attendance. Value impact over hours worked. Trust the talent you hired And watch them flourish.
